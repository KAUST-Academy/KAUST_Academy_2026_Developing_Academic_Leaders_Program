PROGRAM SCHEDULE

Weekly Breakdown

Program Base: Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists (RCOG), 10-18 Union Street, London SE1 1SZ

Sun 29 Mar: Welcome & Introductions

Evening welcome reception and dinner at Shangri-La The Shard, Level 34. Led by Prof Arwa Al-Aama, Dr Sultan Albarakati & Prof Naeemullah Khan.

Mon 30 Mar: Foundations of Academic Leadership

Leading Self, Developing High Performing Academic Teams, and team charter work. Facilitated by Dr Tom Kennie, Dr Jonathan Grant & Dr Sara Hurley.

Tue 31 Mar: Global Insights & Connections

University visits (KCL, QMUL, ASU London, Trinity Laban, UEL), Science Gallery reception, and overview of London Higher's New Frontier Report.

Wed 1 Apr: Leading Strategically & Action Learning

Horizon scanning, scenario thinking, strategic case studies, and facilitated action learning sets. Facilitated by Dr Tom Kennie, Dr Jonathan Grant & Dr Sara Hurley.

Thu 2 Apr: Leading Academic Change & Social Purpose

Leading academic and professional change, UK-KSA HE connections, New Power and the social purpose of universities. Closing comments and next steps.

Fri 3 Apr: Free Time & Departures

Final conversations with peers and facilitators. Free time and travel arrangements.
