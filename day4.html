Leading Strategically and Action Learning

Leading Strategically & Action Learning

A deep dive into strategic leadership — horizon scanning, sense-making, scenario thinking, and strategic case studies — followed by facilitated action learning sets where participants work on real challenges in small groups.

Lead Facilitators: Dr Tom Kennie, Dr Jonathan Grant, Dr Sara Hurley, Prof Sam Smidt & Dr Rebecca Lindner

Location: Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists (RCOG), 10-18 Union Street, London SE1 1SZ
