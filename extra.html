Resources & Reading List

Curated by Dr Tom Kennie, Dr Jonathan Grant & Dr Sara Hurley

The following list of documents are offered as a sample of resources for those who wish to dive deeper into the themes explored during the workshop sessions. They are not intended to be fully comprehensive — merely to enable you to get started on curating your own compendium of leadership literature which may be of value in your role.

The Leadership Travellers Guide

An excellent resource curated by Professor Heiko Spallek, Chief Executive of the Bureau of Health Information (NSW Health, Australia) and former Dean of the Dental School at the University of Sydney.

His free weekly blog on leadership is a superb source of current thinking on many aspects of leadership. We highly recommend you subscribe and bring it to the attention of other leaders in your organisations.
