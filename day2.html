Foundations of Academic Leadership, Leading Self & Developing High Performing Academic Teams

Foundations of Academic Leadership

A full workshop day exploring the foundations of academic leadership, the role of Dean, personal leadership styles, and building high-performing academic teams. Includes a team charter exercise and research on high-performing research teams.

Lead Facilitators: Dr Tom Kennie, Dr Jonathan Grant & Dr Sara Hurley

Location: Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists (RCOG), 10-18 Union Street, London SE1 1SZ
